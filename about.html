About Me

Hi, I'm Donfack Fortune! After graduating from ENSET, I have combined my training as a mechanical engineer and educator to address diverse technical challenges within Cameroon's industrial sector. My expertise lies in Mechanical engineering design and Finite Element Analysis (FEA), which I apply to validate and optimize mechanical systems contributing to improved efficiency and reliability in the diverse sectors.

In addition to my work as a Mechanical engineer, I serve as a teacher at the Technical High School Fongo Tongo, where my focus is on improving education in Cameroon, making Technical education more accessible and fun, and developing sustainable solutions that support local communities.

I address the challenges of today by making tangible, user-centric solutions that bridge the gap between engineering theory and practical application.
