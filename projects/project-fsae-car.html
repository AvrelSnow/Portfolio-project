Formula FSAE Car Design

Project Overview

This project involved the complete design and development of a Formula SAE (FSAE) race car, carried out entirely from scratch in full compliance with Formula SAE competition rules and regulations. The scope covered every major phase of the vehicle design process — including structural 3D modeling, aerodynamic optimization, safety analysis, and regulatory validation — ensuring both performance efficiency and adherence to international standards.

This was a solo project, demonstrating my ability to independently manage complex engineering tasks from concept to simulation. Due to limited funding, the physical fabrication of the prototype was not completed; however, all design and analytical stages were executed with precision and professional rigor.
