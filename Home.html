I am Donfack Fortune, a mechanical engineer and government-trained technical educator, graduating from ENSET.

I specialize in mechanical design and finite element analysis (FEA), using simulation-driven methods to validate, optimize, and improve the efficiency and reliability of mechanical systems across industrial applications in Cameroon.

Alongside my engineering work, I serve as a technical high school teacher at Fongo Tongo Technical High School, where I focus on making technical education more practical, accessible, and engaging, while supporting sustainable, community-oriented solutions.

My work bridges engineering theory and real-world application, with a strong interest in impactful projects at the intersection of transport, agriculture, and education.

Beyond work, I actively teach CAD in university amphitheaters (ENSET Douala and the University of Dschang)and online in the Douala city solidwork user group, volunteer within the TKC community, and continuously develop my public speaking and leadership skills.

Junior Mechanical Engineer

Cameroon Railway Company (CAMRAIL) operates and maintains the national railway network, delivering safe and efficient freight and passenger rail transport across Cameroon. It connects major cities and economic corridors while enabling the movement of bulk goods such as minerals and agricultural products, supporting national trade, mobility, and industrial development.
